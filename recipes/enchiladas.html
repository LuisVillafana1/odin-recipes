Enchiladas

Description

This is one of the best enchiladas you will ever taste. You will be amazed of this delicious plate.

This recipe includes: hand made tortillas, salt, red salsa onion, lemon, jalapenos, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Hand made tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Jalapenos
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Red salsa

Steps

  1. Fry chorizo
  2. Add salt
  3. Prepare hand made tortillas
  4. Add cooked beef meat on top of the tortilla
  5. Add cilantro
  6. Add onion
  7. Add salsa
  8. Time to EAT!
