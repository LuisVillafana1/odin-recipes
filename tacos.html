Tacos

Description

This is one of the best tacos you will ever taste. You will be amazed of this delicious plate.

This recipe includes: hand made tortillas, beef, salt, onion, lemon, cilantro , and salsa.

Ingredients

  • Hand made tortillas
  • Beef
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Fry beef
  2. Add salt
  3. Prepare hand made tortillas
  4. Add cooked beef meat on top of the tortilla
  5. Add cilantro
  6. Add onion
  7. Add salsa
  8. Time to EAT!
