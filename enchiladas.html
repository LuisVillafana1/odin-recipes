Enchiladas

Description

This is one of the best enchiladas you will ever taste. You will be amazed of this delicious plate.

This recipe includes: hand made tortillas, salt, red salsa onion, lemon, jalapenos, tomatoes, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Hand made tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Jalapenos
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Red salsa

Steps

  1. Fry hand made tortillas with red salsa
  2. Add salt
  3. Add cheese on fried tortilla
  4. Add sliced onion on top of enchiladas
  5. Add jalapenos
  6. Add sliced tomatoes
  7. Time to EAT!
